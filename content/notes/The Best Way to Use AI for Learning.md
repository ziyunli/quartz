[The Best Way to Use AI for Learning](https://medium.com/heptabase/the-best-way-to-use-ai-for-learning-762c3467bdf1#fe23) Alan Chan describes his approach to use AI for learning, with example of using Heptabase, a tool built by the company he founded.

The core problem he (and his tool) is trying to solve is

> how to become capable of learning knowledge that is more complex, abstract, and challenging.

The approach he proposed is to:

> ...use AI to engage directly with the best academic textbook in the field for 20 hours.

The steps are roughly:

> Step 1: Parse the PDF
> Step 2: Create the Learning Materials
> Step 3: Read from the Whiteboard and Discuss with AI
> Step 4: Take Notes in Your Own Words
> Step 5: Visualize, Synthesize
